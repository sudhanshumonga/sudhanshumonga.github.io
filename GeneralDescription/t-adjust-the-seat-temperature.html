Adjust the seat temperature

Everyone likes to have a seat at a setting that is most comfortable to them and the seat coolness or warmth is fully configurable.

There is a minimum and maximum defined by your administrator to ensure seats are not uncomfortable.

  1. Launch your personal Primary Control dialog.
  2. Select Seat Configuration.
  3. Tap Layout.
  4. Tap Temperature.
  5. Adjust the temperature up or down.

The temperature blinks until the new setting is reached at which point the temperature remains steady.
