Converting the seats to a reclined sleeping position can be done at the push of a button.

  1. Launch your personal Primary Control dialog.
  2. Select Seat Configuration.
  3. Tap Layout.
  4. Tap Recline to Lie Flat Bed.

The seat slowly reclines to a fully flat bed.

The seat reclines slowly to a comfortable sleeping angle.

Adjust the lighting for your personal pod area based on the desired brightness when resting.
